Meet Dr. Lessner

Dr. Cory M. Lessner’s extensive training and expertise along with the professional team at Millennium Laser Eye Centers are recognized as a LASIK Leader in the United States. Here’s why:

  • 28 years exclusively dedicated to refractive eye surgery.
  • Dr. Lessner was one of the first in the country to use the Alcon Allegretto FS200/EX500 Laser Suite.
  • Dr. Lessner has performed the procedure on his own family, other ophthalmologists, optometrists, surgeons, as well as his staff and staffs family members.
  • Dr. Lessner has treated many of your favorite Miami Dolphins, Miami Heat and Miami Marlins players.
